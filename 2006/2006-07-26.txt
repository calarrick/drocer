Resolution No. 294-06.
By Director Dumas.
Whereas, Board of Control Resolution No. 247-06, adopted June 21,
2006, approved Dell Marketing LP,
based upon its April 27, 2006 quotation, as the firm to be employed by
contract for two years to provide
professional services to maintain six
Dell servers that support the PeopleSoft financial management system, for the Department of Finance;
and
Whereas, because Dell Marketing
LP’s quotation has expired by its
terms, Dell reissued its quotation on
July 14, 2006 to extend the date until
which its pricing is valid to July 27,
2006; now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland that
Resolution No. 247-06, adopted by
this Board June 21, 2006, approving
Dell Marketing LP as the firm to be
employed by contract for two years
to provide professional services necessary to maintain six Dell servers
that support the PeopleSoft management system, for the Department
of Finance, is amended by changing
Dell’s quotation date to July 14,
2006.
Be it further resolved that all
other provisions of Resolution No.
247-06 not expressly amended above
shall remain unchanged and in full
force and effect.
