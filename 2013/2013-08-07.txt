Resolution No. 442-13.
By Director Smith.
Whereas, Board of Control Resolution No. 372-13, adopted July 3,
2013, authorized the Director of Port
Control to enter into contract with
Standard Parking Corporation to
provide professional services necessary to operate the Airport Valet
Service at Cleveland Hopkins International Airport, for the Department
of Port Control; and
Whereas, Resolution No. 372-13
incorrectly stated Standard Parking
Corporation’s concession fee as the
greater of a Minimum Annual Guarantee of $550,000.00 or 60% of Gross
Revenue and 10% of all supplemental revenues collected from ancillary
services over the course of the
Agreement including any option
years; now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland that
Resolution No. 372-13, adopted by
this Board July 3, 2013, authorizing
the Director of Port Control to enter
into contract with Standard Parking
Corporation for the above mentioned
service, is amended by deleting the
concession fee as stated and substituting “the greater of a Minimum
Annual Guarantee of $500,000.00 or
60% of Gross Revenue and 10% of
all supplemental revenues collected
from ancillary services over the
course of the Agreement and including any option years,” where appearing in the resolution.
Be it further resolved that all
other provisions of Resolution No.
372-13 not expressly amended above
shall remain unchanged and in full
force and effect.
