2007, the City of Cleveland, through
the
Director
of
Public
Safety,
entered into an agreement with
W.A.R.N., LLC., City Contract No.
68862, for a period of one year with
one option to renew for an additional one-year period, for professional services necessary to support,
acquire, maintain, and update the
Wide Area Rapid Notification message alerting system for the various
divisions within the Department of
Public Safety; and
Whereas, division (d) of Section
181.102 C.O. authorizes a director to
enter into an agreement with a software vendor for professional services necessary to implement or

support services necessary to maintain the Wide Area Rapid Notification message alerting system for
one year starting July 1, 2013; now,
therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland that
under division (e) of Section 181.102
C.O., the compensation to be paid for
maintenance and technical support
services to be performed under the
agreement with W.A.R.N., LLC is
fixed at an amount not to exceed
$140,000.00.

120-08-070 located at 1461 East 111th

Whereas, Section 183.021 of the

certain specified conditions have
been met, to lease Land Reutilization Program parcels; and
Whereas, Ronald Collins has proposed to the City to lease and develop the parcel for a community garden; and

exist:

of it;

violation of the Building and Housing Code; now, therefore,

Ordinances of Cleveland, Ohio, 1976,
the Commissioner of Purchases and
Supplies is authorized, when directed by the Director of Community
Development, and the Mayor is

for the lease and development of
Permanent
Parcel
No.
120-08-070
located at 1461 East 111th Street,
according to the Land Reutilization
Program in such manner as best carries out the intent of the program.

which amount is determined to be

of the parcel for uses according to
