Drive, thereto, to cause payment of

Whereas, this ordinance constitutes an emergency measure providing for the usual daily operation of
a municipal department; now, therefore
Be it ordained by the Council of
the City of Cleveland:
Section 1. That Section 1 of Ordinance No. 1004-91, passed July 24,
1991, as amended by Ordinance No.
324-97, passed June 2, 1997 and Ordinance No. 2018-2000, passed December 18, 2000, is hereby amended to
read as follows:

The City Record
Section 1. That it is declared to be
in the public interest that the consent of the City be and such consent
is hereby given to the Director of
Transportation of the State of Ohio
to participate in the design and construction of the following improvement in accordance with plans, specifications and estimates approved by
