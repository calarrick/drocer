Section 523.111 Restoration of Electrical Service
If electrical service has been disconnected at a property for a period of
12 (twelve) consecutive months or
more, a customer seeking to have the
electrical service restored at that
property must obtain written verification from the Director of Building and
Housing after inspection of the property by said director or his designee,
that such property is in satisfactory
condition for resumption of electrical
service. Only after receipt of said
written verification may the Division
of Cleveland Public Power restore
electrical service to a property.

Section 535.17 Restoration of Water
Service
If water service has been disconnected at a property for a period of 12
(twelve) consecutive months or more,
a customer seeking to have the water
service restored at that property must
obtain written verification from the
Director of Building and Housing
after inspection of the property by
said director or his designee, that
such property is in satisfactory condition for resumption of water service.
Only after receipt of said written verification may the Division of Water
restore water service to a property.

of an existing emergency in which
case entry may be made at any time
