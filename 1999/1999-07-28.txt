Resolution No. 464-99.
By Director Carmody.
Whereas, pursuant to the authority of Ordinance No. 1744-97 and 52199, passed by the Council of the City
of Cleveland on October 29, 1997 and
March 29, 1999 respectively, and
Board of Control Resolution No. 31999, adopted on May 26, 1999, the City,
through its Director of Finance
entered into City Contract No. 54376

1567

additional services; now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland,

amendment to the agreement between the City of Cleveland and
Maximation LLC, Contract No. 54376,
