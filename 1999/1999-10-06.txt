the

of
life
throughout the neighborhoods of the
City of Cleveland; and
Whereas, unsightly trash and broken bottles that litter the streets,

the streets, sidewalks and other public areas of the City present safety
hazards to residents, particularly
children, and their property; and

distributors can help remedy this
problem by bottling and selling beer
in plastic and other non-breakable
containers, rather than in glass bottles; now, therefore,

the City of Cleveland:

City of Cleveland urges beer manufacturers and distributors to bottle

at the Cleveland Browns Stadium.

City of Cleveland urges beer manufacturers and distributors to double
the percentage of its product sold
for carry-out in the City of Cleveland in non-breakable containers,
such as aluminum and plastic.

City of Cleveland urges manufacturers and distributors of beer to
