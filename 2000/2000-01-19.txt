Resolution No. 11-00.
By Director Balraj.
Whereas, pursuant to the authority of Ordinance No. 629-96, passed by
the Council of the City of Cleveland
on June 10, 1996, and Board of Control Resolution No. 706-96, adopted
on October 9, 1996, the City through
its Director of Port Control entered
into City Contract No. 50433 with
Osborn Engineering Co. (“Consultant”) for peer review of engineering design of the new parking structure and an analysis of the structural condition of the existing parking structure at Cleveland Hopkins
International Airport; and
Whereas, the City has determined
to modify the scope of work to
include the additional inspection
services necessary for professional
design support; and

41

Whereas, Consultant has proposed
by its Design Services Contract
Amendment to perform the additional
services
necessary;
now,
therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland that
the Director of Port Control is hereby authorized to enter into a first
amendment to the agreement between the City of Cleveland and
Osborn Engineering Co. (“Consultant”), Contract No. 50433, for an
addition to the work scope consisting of professional design support
as set forth in Consultant’s Design
Services Contract Amendment. The
amount to be paid for all services
shall be increased by an amount not
to exceed thirty nine thousand seven
hundred sixty three dollars and
twenty six cents ($39,763.26), for a
total contract amount not to exceed
Two Hundred Fifty Nine Thousand
Seven Hundred Sixty Three Dollars
and Twenty Six Cents ($259,763.26).
Be it further resolved that all
other provisions of said Resolution
No. 706-96 not expressly amended
hereby shall remain unchanged and
in full force and effect.

Directors Brown, Dalrat, Owens,
Directors Whitlow, Guzman, Jackson, Hudecek, Patterson, Warren,
Axelrod.
