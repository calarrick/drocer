By Director Smith.
Whereas, under the authority of
Ordinance No. 710-95, passed by the
Council of the City of Cleveland on
June 12, 1995, and Board of Control
Resolution No. 235-03, adopted April
23, 2003, the City through its Director of Port Control, entered into City
Contract No. 61324 with Reed &
Associates, LLC for financial advisory services; and
Whereas, under Board of Control
Resolution Nos. 417-04, 547-05 and
340-06, adopted July 7, 2004, September 28, 2005 and August 9, 2006,
respectively, the City entered into
first, second and third modifications
to City Contract No. 61324 for additional financial services; and
Whereas, under Board of Control
Resolution No. 163-06, adopted May
10, 2006, the City acknowledged the
name change of Reed & Associates,
LLC to Reed, Mandes & Associates,
LLC (“Reed”); and
Whereas, by letter dated March 9,
2007 Reed, Mandes & Associates,
LLC requested the City’s consent to
assign all of Reed Mandes & Associates, LLC interests and certain
obligations under City Contract No.
61324 to Peregrine Advisors, LLC
(“Peregrine”); and
Whereas, Peregrine proposes to
undertake all of Reed’s interests
and all of its obligations first occurring after the effective date of
assignment under City Contract No.
61324,
without
waiving
any
of
Reed’s obligations or the City’s
rights under the contract against
Reed prior to the effective date of
assignment; now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland that
pursuant to the request by Reed,
Mandes & Associates, LLC by its
March 9, 2007 letter, this Board consents to the assignment of City Contract No. 61324 to Peregrine Advisors, LLC effective June 25, 2007.
Be it further resolved that the
Director of Port Control is authorized to execute all documents and
to do all things necessary and
appropriate to implement the consent authorized, provided that the
terms of such assignment do not
conflict with the terms and conditions of City Contract No. 61324.
Be it further resolved, by the Board
of Control of the City of Cleveland
that the employment of the following subconsultant by Peregrine Advisors, LLC is approved:
