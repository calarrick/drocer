Resolution No. 252-09.
By Director Dumas.
Whereas, under the authority of
Section 181.101 of the Codified Ordinances of Cleveland, Ohio, 1976 and
Board of Control Resolution No. 18709 adopted May 20, 2009, the City of
Cleveland entered into City Contract No. 69208 with National Coin
Op, Inc. dba American Pride Car
Wash, for an estimated quantity of
car, van and truck washing services, item nos. 1, 2 and 3 (District
4), for various divisions of City
government, for one year with an
option to renew for one additional
year; and
Whereas, by its June 10, 2009 letter, National Coin Op, Inc. dba
American Pride Car Washes East
(“National”) notified the City that A
M Car Wash Services, Inc. is a related service corporation employing all
employees of National Coin Op, Inc.
dba America Pride Car Washes East
at all locations and, as such, is the
firm named on the Bureau of Workers Compensation Certificate of Premium Payment, liability insurance
policy, and other such documents
pertaining to National; now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Board of
Control of the City of Cleveland
that this Board acknowledges A M
Car Wash Services, Inc., a service
corporation related to National Coin
Op, Inc. dba America Pride Car
Washes East, Contractor under City
Contract No. 69208, as employer of
all National employees at all locations and acting on behalf of
National for all employment-related
matters, including, but not limited
to, payroll, its Bureau of Workers
Compensation Certificate of Premium Payment, and its liability insurance.
Be it further resolved that the
Director of Finance is authorized to
execute and accept all documents in
the name of A M Car Wash Services,
Inc. pertaining to employees and
employment-related matters of National on behalf of the latter, and to
do any acts necessary to acknowledge the relationship between the
corporations for all purposes relevant to City Contract No. 69208.
Yeas: Directors Triozzi, Dumas,
Acting Director Withers, Directors
Smith, Wasik, Carroll, Flask, Cox,
Rush, Hutchinson, Acting Director
Thornton, Director Fumich, Interim
Director
