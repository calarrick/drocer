FIRST READING EMERGENCY
ORDINANCE REFERRED
Ord. No. 3-09.
By
Council
Members
Kelley,
Cleveland and Sweeney (by departmental request).
An emergency ordinance to repeal Section 3 of Ordinance No. 86108, passed July 2, 2008; to renumber existing Section 4 of that ordinance to new Section 3; and to
repeal Ordinance No. 1873-08, passed December 8, 2008, relating to
obstructions within the Harbor and
relating penalty.
Whereas, on July 2, 2008, this
Council passed Ordinance No. 861-08,
which authorized amendments to
Sections 573.02, 573.04, 573.05, 573.10,
and 573.99 relating to obstructions
within the harbor and its relating
penalty; and
Whereas, Section 3 of Ordinance
No. 861-08 contained language which
reverted Sections 573.02 and 573.04
back to their original, unamended
versions unless Council passed additional legislation by December 31,
2008; and
Whereas, Ordinance No. 1873-08,
passed December 8, 2008, amended
Ordinance No. 861-08 to extend the
authority of the amended versions
of Sections 573.02 and 573.04 until
June 30, 2009; and
Whereas, it is the intention of this
Council, by repealing Section 3 of
Ordinance No. 861-08, which contained
the December 31, 2008 reversionary
date, and by repealing Ordinance No.
1873-08, which extended the reversionary date to June 30, 2009, to permanently enact Sections 573.02 and
573.04 as authorized in Section 1 of
Ordinance No. 861-08, passed July 2,
2008, effective July 7, 2008; and
Whereas, this ordinance constitutes
an emergency measure providing for
the usual daily operation of a municipal department; now, therefore,
Be it ordained by the Council of
the City of Cleveland:
Section 1. That Section 3 of Ordinance No. 861-08, passed July 2, 2008
is repealed.
Section 2. That existing Section 4
of Ordinance No. 861-08, passed July
2, 2008 is renumbered to new “Section 3”.
Section 3. That Ordinance No. 187308, passed December 8, 2008, is repealed.
Section 4. That this ordinance is
declared to be an emergency measure and, provided it receives the
affirmative vote of two-thirds of all
the members elected to Council, it
shall take effect and be in force
immediately upon its passage and
approval by the Mayor; otherwise it
shall take effect and be in force
from and after the earliest period
allowed by law.
Referred to Directors of Port Control, Finance, Law; Committees on
Aviation and Transportation, Legislation, Finance.
